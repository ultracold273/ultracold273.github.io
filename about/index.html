关于我

Currently I am woking on the IoT security. I’m familiar with the architecture of various computing systems and the operating systems. I’m also interested in the security of AI algorithms and systems.

Although I was not in the academia anymore, I would like to keep the “academic” introduction below only for archiving.

Previously I obtained my Ph.D from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2018 and B.S degree from Nanjing University in 2012. I did research in the domain of hardware security and security of AI systems. Below lists my publication in the years of 2013 to 2019.
