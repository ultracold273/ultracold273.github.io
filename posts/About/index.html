About me

Bio

I obtained my Ph.D degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2018. My major research interests lay in the area of hardware security, system security and secure architecture for AI systems. Before that I got my B.S in Electronic Science and Engineering from Nanjing University in 2012.

Though I'm not in academia anymore, I would like keep my academic profile here for archiving purpose. Below lists my publication from 2013 to 2019.
